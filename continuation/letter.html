Dear Tanisha,

I have found the one - you.
An extraordinary soul who sees the world through a lens that turns the ordinary into something beautiful. Your perspective gives life a poetic rhythm, making even the mundane feel special.


I am in love with not just your flowers but also your roots - the depth of your thoughts, the richness of your soul. You find joy in deep conversations, on the unnoticed details of life, existence, the subtle nuances of human nature and the mysteries of God. Through your words, I see what I once overlooked.


You love to walk, to bask in the golden light of the sun. Evening strolls , with gentle breeze on a well-lit path brings you peace, and in those moments, I find peace in your presence.


You are like the crashing waves of the sea and the still waters of the lake. A gentle breeze caressing my face, your presence is both calming and exhilarating, a paradox that I find myself endlessly drawn to.


I like the way you capture the beautiful, the sad, the bad, the happy and the mesmerizing moments in life - journaling them to your mind, reflecting on them and embracing their goodness.


I cherish our conversations, our laughter, and our silly dances. With you even the most silly things become infinitely more meaningful. In short, main tanne se pyar hove.


With all my heart,
Namsa Caleb Jajo
